## Getting Started
AttendIn is designed to make universtiy attendance seamless using your smartphone. Instead of paper sign-in sheets, you simply tap a button when you are in the classroom.

Prerequisites
- Device: IOS or Android Smartphone.
- Connectivity: Active Internet connection required
- Permissions: You must allow **Location Services** for AttendIn to verify you are in the classroom.

## Account Setup
#### Creating a New Account
If you are a new student, you will need to register before you can join classes.
1. Open the app and tap Sign Up on the Welcome screen.
2. Enter your Full Name, University Email, and a secure Password.
3. Read and agree to the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy
4. Tap the Sign Up button. You will be logged in and directed to the Home Screen.

#### Logging In
1. Tap Log In on the Welcome screen.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Tap Log In.

#### Forgot Password
If you cannot access your account:
1. Tap Forgot Password on the Login screen.
2. Enter your email address.
3. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password

## Marking Attendance
The Home Screen is your main dashboard. It automatically detects which class you are currently attending based on the time and day.
#### How to check in
1. Arrive at Class: Ensure you are physically inside the classroom.
2. Open Attendin: The app will highlight your current class card at the bottom of the screen.
3. Tap "Mark Attendance":
    - if you are in the correct location and time window, the button will turn green.
    - Note: If you are not in the correct location, the app will say "Out of Location"

## Viewing Your Schedule
To see your full list of classes for the semester:
1. Tap the Calendar Icon in the bottom navigation bar to open the Schedule Tab.
2. You will see a list of all your enrolled classes.
3. Each card displays:
    - Course Name
    - Location
    - Time

## Tracking Your History
Want to check how many classes you've missed?
1. Go to your Schedule tab.
2. Tap on any Class Card to open the Class Details screen.
3. Missed Counter: A red box at the top will tell you how many sessions you have missed
4. Calendar View: Below there is a calendar, Red indicates absences

## Settings & Customization
You can personalize your app experience in the Profile tab.
#### Changing the Theme
Attendin supports Light, Dark, and System themes.
1. Go to Profile > Settings.
2. Tap the dropdown next to Theme Mode.
Select Dark, Light, or System.

#### Changing Your Password
1. Go to Profile > Settings > Password Manager.
2. Enter your Current Password.
3. Enter and confirm your New Password.
4. Tap Change Password to save.

#### Logging Out
1. Go to Profile and tap Logout.
2. A confirmation window will appear. Tap Yes, Logout to securely sign out of your account.


## FAQ & Troubleshooting
**Q: Why is the "Mark Attendance" button disabled?**
A: The button is only active during scheduled class time. If the class hasn't started yet, or if it has already ended, the button will not work

**Q: I am in the classroom, but it says "Out of Location"**
A: Please ensure your phone's Location Services (GPS) are turned on. Try moving closer to the center of the room or near a window for better signal.

**Q: Can I edit my profile picture?**
A: To edit your profile picture go to the Profile tab and tap on the pencil near your picture

**Q: I forgot to mark attendance, can I do it later?**
A: No. Attendin requires real time check ins. If you forget, please speak to your instructor to manually adjust your record.
